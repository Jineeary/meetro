Feature Showcase

Experience the cutting-edge features of Meetro and make meeting friends effortless, efficient, and exciting!

Main Features

1. Midpoint Subway Recommendations

Meetro intelligently calculates the optimal subway station for meetups based on user locations and nearby attractions.

  • Wide-range midpoint calculation with flexible scoring.
  • Prioritizes stations with popular landmarks.
  • Real-time data updates for accurate recommendations.

2. Nearby Attractions

Get personalized recommendations for attractions near the suggested midpoint, making your meetup fun and memorable.

  • Dynamic data reflecting current trends and preferences.
  • Includes amusement parks, shopping malls, and local gems.
  • Filter attractions by category to suit group interests.
