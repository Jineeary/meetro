Main Features

A comprehensive view of Seoul's subway system utilized by Meetro.

1. Midpoint Subway Recommendations

Meetro intelligently calculates the best subway station for meetups, considering user preferences and nearby attractions.

  • Dynamic midpoint calculations tailored to group preferences.
  • Attraction scoring for landmarks and popular spots.
  • Real-time updates using reliable mapping APIs.

2. Nearby Attractions

Explore curated recommendations for attractions near the suggested midpoint to enhance your meetup experience.

  • Prioritized suggestions based on popularity and relevance.
  • Filters for categories like shopping, dining, and entertainment.
  • Up-to-date data reflecting current trends.
